Reaction Time Lab

Sound vs Light Stimulus

This lab tests your reaction time to different stimuli:

  • Visual stimulus: Screen changes from black to red
  • Auditory stimulus: Bell sound plays on black screen

Lab Protocol:

  1. 2 practice rounds (1 visual, 1 auditory)
  2. 6 test rounds (3 visual, 3 auditory - randomized)
  3. Press SPACEBAR as quickly as possible when you detect the stimulus

Total: 8 rounds

Practice Round 1 - Visual Test

Test Type: Visual

Grace Period: 10-15 seconds

Instructions:

Watch the screen carefully. It will change from black to red.

Press SPACEBAR immediately when you see the color change.

Test Disqualified

⚠️ You pressed the spacebar too early!

Please wait for the stimulus before responding.

This round will be repeated.
